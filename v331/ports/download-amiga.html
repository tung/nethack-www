--- layout: legacy title: Amiga Downloads legacy: back_link: text: NetHack 3.3.1 Downloads url: ../downloads.html --- This binary runs on any Amiga with AmigaDOS 3.0 or later. We have had reports that it still works on systems as early as AmigaDOS 2.05.

  • Step 1: download.

    Click on the appropriate link below to download.

    Depending on your browser, you may need to right-click so that the file is saved to disk and not displayed.

    nh331am000.lha(about 1.2M)
    NetHack3.3.1 for Amigas with 68000 or 68010
    nh331am020.lha(about 1.2M)
    NetHack3.3.1 for Amiga with 68020 and up

  • Step 2: unpack.

    Change directory to a new directory and then unpack with:
    lha x nh331amXXX.lha   (XXX is 000 or 020)

  • Step 3: install.

    Read the Guidebook for a general description of what NetHack is and how to play.
    Edit nethack.cnf to adjust your settings preferences.
    Assign NetHack: to the NetHack directory created in step 2.
    Add NetHack: to your path.

  • Step 4: run.

    Run nethack

    Refer to the file readme, the comments in nethack.cnf and the GuideBook and Install.ami within the lha-archive file for further instructions.
